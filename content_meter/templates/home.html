Know-GPT : Detecting AI-Generated Content

About Know-GPT

Know-GPT is a powerful tool that allows you to detect AI-generated content and calculate the percentage of AI content in a given text. Whether you're curious about the authenticity of an article, blog post, or any other piece of text, this tool can provide valuable insights.

Understanding the Results

The Know-GPT AI Content Meter provides a visual representation of the AI content percentage in your text. The meter value indicates the level of AI content detected, with 0% representing no AI content and 100% representing entirely AI-generated content.
